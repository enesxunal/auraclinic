2. What data we collect

3. Purposes and legal bases

We process data to:

  • respond to enquiries and send your analysis report;
  • schedule consultations and provide our services;
  • operate and secure the website;

Processing is based on your consent (forms), contract performance, legitimate interests (website security), or legal duty where applicable.

4. Storage duration

Enquiry data is kept only as long as needed to handle your request and any follow-up care, unless longer retention is required by law. Server logs are rotated regularly.

5. Recipients

Data may be processed by hosting and email providers acting on our instructions. We do not sell personal data. Transfers outside Georgia occur only with appropriate safeguards if required.

6. Your rights

Under Georgian data-protection law you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing, and to withdraw consent. Contact us at info@auraclinicge.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Service of Georgia.

7. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures (HTTPS where available, access controls, limited retention) to protect your data.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The current version is always published on this page.
